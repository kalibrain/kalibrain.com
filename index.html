I’m Kali, a software developer with a passion for making life (and work) a little easier—one line of code at a time. Thanks for stopping by my corner of the internet!

By day, I live in the lovely city of Ann Arbor, Michigan, where I work at the University of Michigan’s Advanced Genomics Core. What do I do there? Glad you asked! I build and improve a software called LIMS (Laboratory Information Management System). Think of it as a supercharged digital assistant for our brilliant lab technicians, helping them decode the mysteries of DNA, cells, and everything in between. Pretty cool, right?
